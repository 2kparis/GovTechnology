Online Public Services (E-Services)

E-Services enable citizens to interact with public administration without physical visits. Services are accessible via portals using secure logins, often integrating multi-factor authentication for security.

Examples

  • NHS appointment booking systems
  • Local council services (e.g., waste collection requests)
  • Renewal of driving licenses and permits online

Benefits

  • Reduces administrative bottlenecks
  • Improves service delivery speed
  • Increases accessibility for citizens with mobility or location issues

    • Challenges

    • Maintaining user-friendly interfaces
    • Ensuring system uptime and reliability
    • Providing support for digital literacy

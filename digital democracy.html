Digital Democracy and Civic Engagement

Digital platforms enhance democratic participation through online voting information, petitions, forums, and consultations. They enable inclusive governance and more representative policy-making.

Examples

  • E-petitions influencing parliamentary debates
  • Online public consultations on housing plans
  • Voting reminders and registration via email or apps

Benefits

  • Increased voter turnout and engagement
  • Empowers younger and tech-savvy citizens
  • Encourages community involvement in policy development

    • Challenges

    • Security concerns in e-voting systems
    • Risk of manipulation or misinformation
    • Digital divide excluding less tech-literate groups
